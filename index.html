About our E-Cell

The capacity and willingness to develop, organize and manage a business venture along with any of its risks in order to make a profit is what distinguishes businessman from an entrepreneur. E-Cell, NIT Silchar or Entrepreneurship Cell of National Institute of Technology, Silchar is a non-profit organization run by the students of NIT Silchar established with the aim to promote entrepreneurship and start-ups within the college. In a world where every man is a company himself by the virtue of his ideas, we thrive to develop the attitude of self-dependency among our college students. Keeping an aim of fostering nurturing the spirit of entrepreneurship in the youth of NIT Silchar, we at E-Cell, NIT Silchar have conceptualized & successfully implemented various initiatives to help students, young entrepreneurs & professionals in their entrepreneurial journey.

Guest Lectures

Pitching Competition

Have you ever had an idea that you wanted to expand upon but didn’t have the necessary tools to develop it? SRiJAN 1.0 and The Entrepreneurship Cell NIT Silchar is on their course to give you a brilliant platform to showcase your ideas and bag those necessary tools.

Board Room

Here's an amazing opportunity for you to actually get acquainted with what a CEO, CTO and COO does.The Board room consists of minimum three and maximum four members from each team. Each team will be given a situation, or a issue, that they need to solve by mutual discussion, and upfront opinions. If you think you're good at problem solving, and can express your opinion confidently, this is just your thing!

Business Model Canvas Competion

“A vision without a strategy remains an illusion.” With the very same perspective, SRiJaN 1.0 is all set to organise an event 'Business model canvas competition' which will facilitate the student entrepreneurs to learn about the strategic management of business and showcase their potentials in this arena.

Stock Market Workshop

We are very much familiar with the term, but the details of Stock Market are a mystery. Through this workshop one will be learn the basics and would be able start exploring this seemingly much attractive domain. A stock market, equity market or share market is the aggregation of buyers and sellers (a loose network of economic transactions, not a physical entity or discrete entity) of stocks (also called shares).

Business Quiz

Do you know the names of all the Unicorn companies that are start-ups? Or is business trivia always on your browser history? Or are you simply one of those people who love connecting dots between random clues? If yes, then SRiJaN 1.0 "Business Quiz" is just the place for you to prove your mettle, while competing with the finest business-enthusiasts from all over the country.

IPL Auction

Does the idea of placing bids and winning them in the heat of the moment against other bidders sound exciting? Then you’ve come to the right place. Welcome to the IPL Auction, an event under the E-Summit 2019: Srijan1.0 where you can put yourself in the shoes of a bidder to build your dream team from scratch to lift the IPL trophy with.
